Surviving Quarantine

Activities, Updates and Advice to help you keep your sanity

Why Quarantine?

Your Health

Coronavirus affects everyone differently, but has the potential to be serious and life-threatening. Many people require hospitalization and significant medical resources. Even if you recover, there are still long-term effects from the virus that are not yet well understood.

Others' Health

There are high-risk people in every community, and you put their lives in danger if you do not recommended precautions. Researchers estimate that 1 in 4 Coronavirus carriers are asymptomatic, so even if you feel fine, you could be spreading the disease and putting the lives of those you love in danger.

The healthcare system

Slowing the spread of COVID-19 helps to lessen the demand on our healthcare system, ensuring that people with medical emergencies can be treated. Additionally, this helps to protect our health care professionals, who are working hard to keep you and your community safe.
