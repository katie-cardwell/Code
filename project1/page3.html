Health and Fitness

How to stay healthy this quarantine

Mental Health

The pandemic has created a stressful and uncertain time for many, so it is important not to neglect your mental health during quarantine! Whether you find your happiness through meditation, yoga, gardening, or another hobby, there are many free resources out there to help you make your mental health a priiority this quarantine.

Exercise

Depending on your situation, you may not be able or allowed to exercise like you normally do. Thankfully, many companies are offering free online classes or workout routines to help you stay fit! Other activities such as a long walk, or even cleaning can help keep you up and active.

Eating

Now is the time to try those healthy recipies! Cooking can be a fun activity to do with your family or on your own. Just be sure to plan your meals ahead of time to limit grocery store runs. If you can, there are other alternatives for meal prep and grocery deliveries to limit your trips outside.
